Journey Through Middle-Earth

Discover the magic of Tolkien's world with authentic guided tours

The Shire

Experience the peaceful life of Hobbits in their rolling green homeland. Visit Bag End, the Green Dragon Inn, and enjoy second breakfast in the most idyllic corner of Middle-Earth.

Rivendell

Discover the hidden valley of the Elves, where time stands still and ancient wisdom flows like the crystal rivers. Meet with Elrond and witness the beauty of the Last Homely House.

Mordor & Mount Doom

For the brave of heart, witness the dark realm of Sauron. Walk where Frodo and Sam completed their perilous quest. Not recommended for first-time visitors to Middle-Earth.

Rohan

Ride with the horse-lords across the vast plains. Experience the golden halls of Edoras and witness the legendary horsemanship of the Rohirrim.

Erebor & The Lonely Mountain

Journey with Thorin's company to reclaim the dwarven kingdom. Explore the halls under the mountain and the treasures of Smaug.
